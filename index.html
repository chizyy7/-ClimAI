    What is ClimAI?

    ClimAI combines live weather data, computer vision, and AI language models to give you intelligent, human-friendly weather insights. Whether you want to know what to wear, whether to carry an umbrella, or simply understand what that ominous sky outside means — ClimAI has you covered.

    Live Data

    Real-time weather from OpenWeatherMap covering 200,000+ cities worldwide, updated every few minutes.

    🧠

    AI Analysis

    Claude Sonnet translates raw weather numbers into clear, practical advice tailored to your location.

    📸

    Image Detection
